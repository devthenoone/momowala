Our Story

About Momowala

From the streets of Delhi to the heart of Doha — this is our story.

MQatar
Who We Are

Authentic Delhi
in Doha

Momowala was born from a simple but powerful idea: bring the true, uncompromised taste of Delhi street food to Qatar's food lovers. We started small, with a deep passion for momos and an obsession with getting the flavours exactly right.

Every recipe is rooted in real Delhi tradition — from the way we steam, fry and grill our momos, to the chutneys and sauces we make in-house. We source fresh chicken and authentic spices directly from India, because shortcuts are not in our vocabulary.

Today we are proud to serve Doha's food community with an experience that is fresh, soulful, and truly unforgettable — one momo at a time.

Abhishek Wasnik
Founder and chief executive
"Every momo we make is a promise — a promise of authenticity, of love, of Delhi."

Momowala was born from a vision, passion for food, and the belief that great ideas come from meaningful connections.

I moved to Doha, Qatar with the intention of working a regular 9-5 job, but deep down I always had a dream of building something of my own. During that time, I spent countless hours researching how businesses operate in Doha, learning the market, and preparing myself for the right opportunity.

During this journey, I met Faizal from Delhi, a well-known food blogger on social media. What started as a simple meeting quickly turned into long conversations about life, ideas, and our shared vision for food. We met several times, often spending hours exchanging thoughts and inspiration.

One day, Faizal called me and shared his vision of the kind of food experience he wanted people in Doha to have. The idea instantly resonated with me. Without wasting any time, we started working on it the very next day.

Combining Faizal's social media reach and loyal community with my business vision and execution, Momowala was born.

Today, Momowala represents our passion for bringing authentic flavors and memorable food experiences to the people of Doha.

Abhishek Wasnik

“Every momo we serve carries the soul of Delhi — crafted with passion, tradition, and love.”

I started Momowala with one dream: to bring the exact taste of Delhi's streets to every food lover in Qatar. Growing up eating momos on the streets of Delhi, I knew what authentic meant — the perfect fold, the right chutney, the smoke from the tandoor.

When I moved to Doha, I missed that taste every single day. So I decided to stop missing it and start serving it. Every day, we wake up to make sure that every single momo that leaves our kitchen is worthy of Delhi's legacy.

We are still growing, still learning — but our commitment to your taste will never change. Thank you Doha, for the love. 🥟🇮🇳
